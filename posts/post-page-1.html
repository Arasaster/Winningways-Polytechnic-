State Officials Explore Winning Ways Polytechnic Ahead of Approval Process

State Officials Explore Winning Ways Polytechnic Ahead of Approval Process Winning Ways Polytechnic welcomed a delegation of state officials on the 5th Of December 2023, led by the Director (Alhaja suleiman M.A) and Deputy Director (Mr Mahmud Waheed) of the Ministry of Tertiary Education.

This visitation, a crucial step prior to state approval, aimed to assess the institution's readiness and align with the standards set by the Kwara state government. The Director and Deputy Director, representing the Permanent Secretary of the Commission, delved into a comprehensive exploration of the campus. Their keen interest in academic offerings, faculty, and infrastructure underscored the government's commitment to ensuring excellence in tertiary education. During their visit, the officials engaged with the Administrative heads of the institution gaining insights into the unique readiness of Winning Ways Polytechnic.

These interactions showcased the institution's commitment to academic excellence and its vibrant, inclusive community. As representatives of the Permanent Secretary, the officials carried the responsibility of ensuring Winning Ways Polytechnic meets state standards. Their presence emphasized the kwara state government's dedication to supporting institutions that contribute significantly to educational advancement. This visitation sets the stage for the approval process, with Winning Ways Polytechnic steadfast in its commitment to meeting and exceeding state expectations. The institution remains poised for success, dedicated to providing exceptional education and contributing to the academic growth of its students.
