Winning Ways Polytechnic Secures State Approval In a momentous stride towards academic recognition

Milestone Achieved: Winning Ways Polytechnic Secures State Approval In a momentous stride towards academic recognition, Winning Ways Polytechnic proudly announces that it has received official state approval on December 7, 2023.

2023. This achievement marks a significant milestone in the institution's journey, solidifying its commitment to providing quality education and meeting the standards set by the Ministry of Tertiary Education. The approval, granted after a thorough assessment by state officials led by the Director and Deputy Director, signifies the alignment of Winning Ways Polytechnic with the government's expectations for excellence in tertiary education. The institution's dedication to academic rigor, vibrant community engagement, and top-notch facilities has been recognized and endorsed by the state.

The entire Winning Ways Polytechnic community celebrates this accomplishment with pride and gratitude. The journey towards state approval has been a collaborative effort contributing to the institution's success. With the official recognition in place, Winning Ways Polytechnic looks forward to a future of continued growth, innovation, and academic distinction. The institution remains committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders and professionals, providing them with an environment conducive to Educative, Entrepreneurial learning and personal development.

As the polytechnic embraces this new chapter, the achievement of state approval, stands as a testament to Winning Ways Polytechnic's dedication to excellence in education and its significant contribution to the advancement of tertiary learning.
