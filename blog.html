Approval Process Winning Ways Polytechnic 05 Dec 2023

State Officials Explore Winning Ways Polytechnic Ahead of Approval Process Winning Ways Polytechnic welcomed a delegation of state officials on the 5th Of December 2023, led by the Director (Alhaja suleiman M.A) and Deputy Director (Mr Mahmud Waheed) of the Ministry of Tertiary Education. This visitation, a crucial step prior to state approval, aimed to assess the institution's readiness and align with the standards set by the Kwara state government. The Director and Deputy Director, representing the Permanent Secretary of the Commission, delved into a comprehensive exploration of the campus.

Winning Ways Polytechnic Secures State Approval In a momentous 07 Dec 2023

Milestone Achieved: Winning Ways Polytechnic Secures State Approval In a momentous stride towards academic recognition, Winning Ways Polytechnic proudly announces that it has received official state approval on December 7, 2023. This achievement marks a significant milestone in the institution's journey, solidifying its commitment to providing quality education and meeting the standards set by the Ministry of Tertiary Education. The approval, granted after a thorough assessment by state officials led by the Director and Deputy Director, signifies the alignment of Winning Ways Polytechnic with the government's expectations for excellence in tertiary education. The institution's dedication to academic rigor, vibrant community engagement, and top-notch facilities has been recognized and endorsed by the state.
